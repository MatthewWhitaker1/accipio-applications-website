Accipio Applications

To Enjoy. To Excite. To Educate.

Who are we?

We are a Young Enterprise team from Ridgewood Sixth Form, Doncaster and are a team of hard working individuals who are striving to not only create a viable and successful business, but also to be the best Young Enterprise team there is.

What we produce:

At Accipio Applications, we produce exciting and enjoyable video games that provide awareness and education about how we can do our best to sustain a healthy environment for us to live in. Our aim for our first game 'Reef Rush' is for it to be fun to play but also allow the user to gain an insight into how we can help our ocean and its wildlife.

Our App:

Here you can see our game 'Reef Rush', a fun and thrilling mobile game available on the Google Play Store. The aim of the game is to avoid all sharks that will be trying to eat you and the hooks from the fisherman trying to fish you, and also watch out for any pesky obsticles in the way whilst you are swimming for your safety. The game gets progressively harder the longer you survive and has different levels of difficulty, depending on how confident a swimmer you are! The higher the difficulty, the faster you swim, and also the better bragging rights to your friends.
